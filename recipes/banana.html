Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread with applesauce is surprisingly moist and has a wonderful
flavor. Add nuts or raisins for variation.

Ingredients

    cooking spray
    1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    ¾ cup white sugar
    1 ¼ teaspoons baking powder
    ½ teaspoon baking soda
    ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
    1 cup banana, mashed
    ¼ cup applesauce
    2 egg whites

Steps

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    Lightly grease an 8x4-inch loaf pan..
    Stir flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda,
    and cinnamon together in a large bowl.
    Add banana, applesauce, and egg whites; stir
    just until combined. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
    Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted
    into the center comes out clean, 50 to 55 minutes.
    Turn out onto a wire rack and allow to cool before slicing.
